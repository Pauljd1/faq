How do I invite my teammates?

You can invite teammates directly from your dashboard by clicking on “Team Settings” and selecting “Invite Members.” Simply enter their email addresses, assign roles, and they’ll receive an invitation link to join your workspace.

How to create new workspace

To create a new workspace, navigate to your dashboard and click on “Create Workspace.” From there, you can set a name, choose your template, and configure access permissions. Each workspace can be customized to fit different projects or teams.e

How do I Set Roles And Permissions?

Roles and permissions can be managed from the “Team Settings” section. You can assign roles like Admin, Editor, or Viewer to control who can edit content, manage users, or view analytics. This ensures that your workspace remains organized and secure.
